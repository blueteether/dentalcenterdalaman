DENTAL CENTER DALAMAN – GDPR INFORMATION NOTICE

As Dental Center Dalaman, in accordance with the Law on the Protection of Personal Data No. 6698 (“KVKK”), we value the security of your personal data and would like to inform you about how your data is processed.

1. Data Controller

Your personal data is processed by Dental Center Dalaman in the capacity of data controller.

2. Personal Data Processed

  • Identity Information (Name, surname, date of birth, etc.)
  • Contact Information (Phone number, email address, address, etc.)
  • Visual and Audio Records (Camera footage, intraoral photographs, etc.)
  • Website Usage Data (IP address, cookie data, etc.)

3. Purposes of Processing Personal Data

  • Carrying out appointment procedures
  • Identity verification and creation of patient records
  • Measuring and increasing patient satisfaction
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

4. Method and Legal Basis of Collecting Personal Data

Your personal data may be collected verbally, in writing, or electronically via our website, phone, email, physical forms, and during clinic visits. This data is processed based on the legal grounds specified in Articles 5 and 6 of the KVKK.

5. Transfer of Personal Data

Your collected personal data is shared only with our authorized personnel for the purpose of evaluating your contact requests and is not shared with third parties.

6. Retention Period of Personal Data

Your personal data will be retained for the periods prescribed by the relevant legislation and will be deleted, destroyed, or anonymized once these periods have expired.

7. Your Rights Under the KVKK

  • To learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • If processed, to request information regarding such processing
  • To learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used in accordance with its purpose
  • To know the third parties to whom it has been transferred within or outside the country
  • To request the correction of incomplete or inaccurate data
  • To request the deletion or destruction of your personal data within the scope of Article 7 of the KVKK
  • To request that the correction or deletion be notified to third parties to whom the data has been transferred
  • To object to any result arising to your detriment as a result of the analysis of the data exclusively through automated systems
  • To demand compensation in case of damage due to unlawful processing
