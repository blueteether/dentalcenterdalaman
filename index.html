Patient Information

1.Do you offer services in English for international patients?

Yes, we provide services in English to ensure clear communication and understanding throughout your treatment process.

2.How can I book an appointment if I’m coming from the UK?

You can easily book an appointment by contacting us via whatsapp or email.

3.Can I get a detailed treatment plan before traveling to Turkey?

Absolutely. We can arrange an online consultation where we’ll discuss your dental needs and provide a detailed treatment plan, including costs and duration, before you travel.

4.Do you provide accommodation assistance for international patients?

We do not offer accommodation services, but we are happy to provide information on nearby hotels and travel options if needed.

Meet
The Dentist

 Dentist İbrahim SÖZER was born in Burdur in 1979. Sözer has completed his high school education in English at Şanlıurfa Anatolian and Burdur Anatolian High School. After that his undergraduate education, has been completed in 2002 with a degree at Ege Universiyty, which he started in 1997 at Gazi University Faculty of Dentistry. He has attended many seminars and courses on cosmetic dentistry and implant surgery. He has been continuing his professional life in his own clinic since 2002. He has been working intensively on implant-supported smile aesthetics. He is married and has a child. He is interested in cycling, kitesurfing, martial arts and diving.
